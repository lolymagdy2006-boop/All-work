Course Overview

This comprehensive course introduces you to the fundamentals of Information Systems. You'll learn how to design, model, and implement effective data systems that support business needs.

Starting with data modeling concepts, we progress through database design, system analysis, and practical implementation techniques. Each lecture builds on the previous one, providing both theoretical foundations and hands-on examples.

Perfect for students, developers, and professionals looking to understand the backbone of modern information systems.
